Lasagna

Description

Come on, you know what lasagna is. Everyone knows what lasagna is. Cats know what lasagna is. Don't be absurd.

Ingredients

  • Italian sausage
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • More garlic
  • Believe it or not, even more garlic
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Even more cheese than there is garlic
  • Probably some tomato sauce; with enough cheese it doesn't really matter

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles
  2. While noodles boil, cut the stuff that needs to be cut
  3. Cook meat so it doesn't poison you
  4. When everything is ready, put it all in a baking dish and bake it until the cheese looks right. You'll know when.
