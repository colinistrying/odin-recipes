Hot Dog

Description

So it's come to this. You want a hot dog and you don't know how to make one, so you needed to look up a recipe. You should be ashamed of yourself, truly.

Ingredients

  • Hot dogs (nobody makes their own hot dogs)
  • Hot dog buns (optional, but strongly encouraged for the sake of not weirding out everyone around you)
  • Whatever you want to put on the hot dog - there's literally no wrong choice

Steps

  1. Boil hot dogs
    Note: You could also grill the hot dogs, but since you looked up a recipe for hot dogs it seems likely you don't know how to do that so let's not burn the house down
  2. If you're using a bun, put the warmed hot dog in the bun
  3. Smother the hot dog with whatever toppings you've selected until the hot dog is buried enough that maybe you'll forget you're eating a hot dog
